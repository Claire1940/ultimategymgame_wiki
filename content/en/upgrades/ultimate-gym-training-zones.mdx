<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Optimize Your Training Zone Routine">

Progressing efficiently through the **ultimate gym training zones** requires a systematic approach. New players should establish a solid strength foundation before attempting to specialize in hypertrophy or advanced calisthenics.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Your Strength Baseline">
    Begin your journey in the Strength Zone. Focus on core compound lifts like the Bench Press, Squat, and Deadlift. Building a strong baseline allows you to handle heavier weights when you transition to other zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Transition to Hypertrophy for Muscle Growth">
    Once your base lifts are stable, move to the Hypertrophy Zone. Utilize Cable Machines and Dumbbells to target specific muscle groups, accelerating visible muscle growth and expanding your character's physical frame.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Incorporate Endurance for Stamina">
    Integrate the Endurance Zone to build stamina. Use bodyweight exercises such as Pull-Ups and Dips. This zone ensures your character can sustain long, high-intensity training sessions without fatiguing rapidly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Advanced Calisthenics">
    Use your combined strength and endurance to progress through the calisthenics pathway. Work your way up from basic movements to elite milestones, culminating in the legendary Maltese exercise.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Track Your Milestones">
