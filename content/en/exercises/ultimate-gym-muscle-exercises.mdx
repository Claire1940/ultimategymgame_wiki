<Section icon={Map} title="Core Training Zones & Mechanics">

To build an impressive physique in the Ultimate Gym Game on Roblox, you must understand where and how to train. The gym is divided into three distinct training zones, each designed around a specific progression goal. Selecting the correct zone before starting your **ultimate gym muscle exercises** determines whether you are building raw power, expanding muscle volume, or boosting your training stamina.

| Training Zone | Primary Goal | Focus Area | Progress Signal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strength** | Lift heavier weights | Max power and PR progression | Higher successful lifting weight |
| **Hypertrophy** | Increase muscle growth | Building visible muscle volume | Continued physical size development |
| **Endurance** | Train for longer periods | Stamina and repeated performance | More reps completed before fatigue |

<Callout type="warning" title="Zone Optimization">
