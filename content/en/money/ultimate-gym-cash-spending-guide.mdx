<Section icon={Shield} title="Training Zone Spending Alignment">

To avoid wasting cash, you must align your purchases with the specific training zone you are working in. Buying an endurance-focused food item while training for a 1RM (One Rep Max) in the Strength zone is a complete waste of resources.

| Training Zone | Desired Outcome | Recommended Purchases | Avoid Buying |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strength** | Heavier barbell lifts, higher PRs | High-protein food, power supplements | Low-calorie stamina snacks |
| **Hypertrophy** | Visible muscle growth, larger character size | Mass gainers, moderate-tier food | Pure endurance energy drinks |
| **Endurance** | Longer sets, calisthenics progression | Stamina food, recovery supplements | Heavy mass-building shakes |

<Callout type="info" title="Kilograms vs. Pounds">
Whether you train in KG or LBS does not affect your cash earnings or buff multipliers. Choose the unit you are most comfortable tracking for personal records.
</Callout>
