Welcome to the **ultimate gym beginner guide**, your roadmap to mastering the mechanics of muscle growth, weight progression, and training efficiency. To succeed in this realistic bodybuilding simulator, you must understand how your daily workout choices translate into visible character growth and higher overhead rank titles. 

The gym is divided into three distinct training zones. Choosing the correct zone ensures your efforts align with your progression goals, whether you want to lift heavier weights, maximize muscle size, or improve repeated-effort stamina.

| Training Zone | Primary Goal | Training Focus | Recommended For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strength** | Lift heavier weights | Maximum strength and personal records | Climbing strength-based leaderboards |
| **Hypertrophy** | Increase muscle growth | Building visible muscle size | Creating a balanced, muscular physique |
| **Endurance** | Train for longer sessions | Sustained exercise performance | Long sessions and high-repetition calisthenics |

<Callout type="tip" title="Training Zone Strategy">
Always select one training zone and stick to it for your entire workout session. Switching zones after every individual exercise slows down your character's adaptation rate and reduces overall progression efficiency.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Gym Progression Route">

To build your character from a complete novice to a high-ranking lifter, follow this structured progression path. This sequence ensures you do not waste hard-earned cash or fail heavy lifts early on.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Training Goal">
    Decide whether your immediate priority is lifting raw weight, growing visible muscle, or building stamina. Head to the corresponding zone (Strength, Hypertrophy, or Endurance) inside the gym to begin your workout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Start with Core Compound Lifts">
    Begin your training session with high-impact compound exercises. Barbell movements such as the Bench Press, Squat, and Deadlift are excellent choices because they target multiple major muscle groups simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Progress Weight Gradually">
    Avoid selecting the heaviest weight plate immediately. Complete successful sets at a manageable weight, set a personal record, and then increase the load incrementally to ensure steady, uninterrupted progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Earn and Reinvest Cash">
    Every completed exercise set awards you cash. Do not let this currency sit idle; visit the gym shop regularly to purchase food, supplements, and shaker upgrades that directly boost your training efficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Mix and Activate Shaker Buffs">
    Combine compatible foods and supplements in your inventory using the shaker system. Consume these mixed buffs right before you enter your target training zone to maximize their active duration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Failed Lifts">
Attempting to lift weights that are far beyond your current strength level leads to failed lifts. Failed attempts waste valuable training time and yield zero progression points. Use your personal records as a guide for safe progression.
</Callout>

</Section>
