<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimizing Training Zones and Exercises">

Understanding how the three distinct training zones interact with the 31+ exercises is key to mastering the game. Each zone applies a unique modifier to your efforts, making certain equipment choices highly optimal for specific goals.

| Training Zone | Growth Multiplier | Recommended Exercises | Progression Signal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength** | Maximum power output | Bench Press, Deadlift, Squat | Higher weight capacity |
| **Hypertrophy** | Increased muscle volume | Dumbbells, Cable Machines | Visible physical growth |
| **Endurance** | Stamina & recovery speed | Pull-Ups, Dips, Calisthenics | Longer continuous sets |

Using this **ultimate gym starting guide** strategy, you should align your active nutrition buffs with the zone you are currently using. For example, do not consume a hypertrophy-boosting shake if you plan to spend your session doing endurance-focused calisthenics.
