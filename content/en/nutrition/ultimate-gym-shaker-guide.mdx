<Section icon={Target} title="Supplement Categories and Mixing Strategies">

To master the **Ultimate Gym Shaker Guide**, you must understand the distinction between food and supplements. Food items typically provide the base "fuel" for your workout, while supplements act as "catalysts" that amplify specific attributes like stamina or power output. The shaker system bridges these two, allowing you to carry a customized "pre-workout" or "recovery" blend in your inventory.

When selecting items at the gym shop, consider your current training zone. The game is divided into three distinct areas: Strength, Hypertrophy, and Endurance. A shake designed for the Strength zone should prioritize items that assist with heavy, low-rep sets on the Squat or Deadlift. Conversely, a Hypertrophy shake should focus on muscle volume and sustained effort for cable and dumbbell exercises.

| Supplement Type | Best Zone Synergy | Progression Signal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mass Gainers** | Hypertrophy | Visible muscle size increases |
| **Power Boosters** | Strength | New Personal Records (PRs) |
| **Stamina Elixirs** | Endurance | Longer training sessions |
| **Recovery Blends** | General | Reduced performance drop-off |
| **Hybrid Mixes** | Mixed | Balanced growth across all stats |

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Buff Waste">
Do not activate your shaker items immediately before logging off or switching activities. Buffs are timer-based; once activated, you must stay focused on your training routine to get the full value of your investment.
</Callout>
