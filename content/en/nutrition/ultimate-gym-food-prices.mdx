<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Shaker System: Mixing for Maximum Efficiency">

The Shaker system is one of the most powerful tools in the game, allowing you to combine different buffs into a single, manageable item. By mixing compatible food and supplements, you can create a custom "stack" that targets your specific needs, whether that is pure strength for a Deadlift PR or endurance for a long Calisthenics session.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Earn and Save Cash">
    Complete your core lifts (Bench, Squat, Deadlift) to build a cash reserve. You will need enough to buy at least two compatible items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase Compatible Items">
    Visit the shop and select items that complement each other. For example, pair a strength-boosting food with a power-focused supplement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use the Shaker Interface">
    Open your inventory and select the Shaker. Place your purchased items into the slots to combine their effects into a single consumable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Before Training">
    Once mixed, the Shaker item stays in your inventory. Activate it only when you are standing in the training zone you intend to use.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Using the Shaker effectively reduces the "menu time" spent during your workout. Instead of stopping to eat and take supplements separately, one use of the Shaker applies all buffs simultaneously. This ensures that the timers for all your bonuses are perfectly synced, maximizing the window of peak performance.

| **Growth Stack** | Hypertrophy Food | Size Supplement | **Fast Muscle Gain** |
| **Stamina Stack** | Endurance Food | Recovery Supplement | **Longer Sessions** |

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Bonus">
Mixing items in the Shaker sometimes provides a slight duration bonus or a more stable buff application compared to taking them individually. Always use the Shaker when you have more than one buff available.
