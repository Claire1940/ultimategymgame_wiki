<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Daily Meal Preparation">

Structuring your daily meals ensures you hit your target macros without relying on fast food or convenience items. Follow this systematic approach to organize your daily nutrition plan.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Calculate Daily Macronutrient Targets">
    Determine your total daily caloric needs. For muscle hypertrophy, aim for a modest caloric surplus of 300 to 500 calories above your maintenance level. Set your protein goal between 1 and 2 grams per pound of body weight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Source and Prep Whole Foods">
    Purchase your proteins, carbohydrates, and vegetables in bulk. Dedicate two days a week to cook your chicken, beef, and rice in advance. This reduces daily prep time and prevents missed meals.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Structure Meal Timing Around Training">
    Allocate your largest carbohydrate meals before and immediately after your workout. This ensures your muscles have adequate glycogen for energy and rapid replenishment during recovery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor and Adjust Weekly">
    Track your body weight and physical changes. If weight gain stalls, increase your daily carbohydrate intake by 50 grams. If fat gain is too rapid, slightly reduce dietary fats while keeping protein constant.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Common Prep Mistakes">
Do not over-complicate your meals with excessive sauces and seasonings that add hidden calories. Keep your food clean, measurable, and easily digestible.
</Callout>

</Section>
